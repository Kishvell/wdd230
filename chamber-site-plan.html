Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

"Powder Magazines" Chamber of Commerce
Reason: This name is inspired on the meaning of my town's name in english, the name in spanish would be "Los Polvorines"

Site Purpose

The "Powder Magazines" Chamber of Commerce website aims to:
1. Promote businesses by providing information about member companies, events, and resources.
2. Connect the community by offering a platform for networking, events, and local news.

Scenarios

Here are some scenarios representing potential user questions:
1. "What are the upcoming business networking events in "Powder Magazines"?"
2. "How can I find a local contractor in "Powder Magazines"?"
3. "What are the main attractions and activities in "Powder Magazines"?"

Color Schema

The site will utilize a color palette reflecting the Chamber's brand:
Primary Color: #007bff (Blue) - Used for headings, buttons, etc.
Secondary Color: #f8f9fa (Light Gray) - Used for backgrounds, body text, etc.
Accent Color: #28a745 (Green) - Used for highlights, links, etc.

Typography

The site will use the following font families:
Heading Font: Arial
Body Font: Verdana

Wireframe

Mobile View:
- Header with logo and navigation menu - Hero image or banner showcasing Powder Magazines - Section for featured events - Section for business directory - Footer with contact information and social media links
Desktop View:
- Header with logo, navigation menu, and search bar - Hero image or banner showcasing Powder Magazines - Two columns: - Left column: Featured events, news articles - Right column: Business directory, membership information - Footer with contact information, social media links, and site map
